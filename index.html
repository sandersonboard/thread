The problem

ECM was built for one person at a time.

Every legacy evidence-collection tool — including the ones we built — assumes a single user uploading to a single web app, with reviewers chasing them by email afterward.

The bot, the control owner, the reviewer, and the auditor each end up with their own slice of the story. None of them see the same thing. The judgment behind every approval gets lost in PDF receipts.

The wedge

The thread is the evidence.

Move the conversation into Slack — where the work already happens — and put Optro ECM into the thread as a first-class participant. The bot surfaces facts, finds linked tickets, summarizes disagreements, and anchors the receipt to chain.

The thread becomes the artifact. Not a PDF after the fact. The conversation IS the audit trail — searchable, attestable, and ready for the next quarter's close.
